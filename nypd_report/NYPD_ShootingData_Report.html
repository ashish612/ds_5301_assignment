NYPD Historical Shooting data analysis

Data Source Information

  • Available on the NYPD website
  • A list of every shooting incident that occurred in NYC going back to 2006 through the end of the 2020.
  • Each record represents a shooting incident in NYC and includes information about the event, the location and time of occurrence

Data Analysis

Data formatting was required before any analysis could be performed. It involved removing missing data, formate dates, and discard unwanted columns.

  • Data contains information about 5 New York Boroughs ( Queens, Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan and Straten Island).
  • 2709 shootings occured in Brooklyn between 2006 and 2020, topping the chart with Bronx following at 1903 shootings during the same period.
  • Most of the shootings perpetrators were identified as black male.
  • Between 2006 and 2019, shooting has gradually gone down in NY
